G9-12 Writing KC Map - Coverage Matrix

Every tested writing capability + every CCSS/TEKS standard family, mapped to its owner: an HS-owned KC, an external HS course (Language/Vocabulary/Reading), or an explicit documented descope.

What this proves, and what it does not. A COVERAGE argument: no tested capability or standard family is left un-owned (necessary for students to pass). NOT an efficacy claim: coverage does not prove the teaching works - that needs field data with real students, which we do not have. Read as "the course targets everything the tests + standards demand, or explicitly defers it," not "proven to produce passing scores."
