## Cross-cutting root cause
The dominant real defect is NOT the re-architecture: it is that some PP100 held-out sources were chosen for
topic variety without checking they match the lesson's TAUGHT genre/skill and difficulty, and a few gate/
assigned-position PP100s conflict with the taught 'you choose' framing. This is a mastery-source-selection
QA gap worth a standing rule (a held-out PP100 source must share the lesson's genre + not demand untaught
moves), independent of fixing these 17 instances.
