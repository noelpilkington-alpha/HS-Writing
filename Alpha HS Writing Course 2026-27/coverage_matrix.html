Grade 10 Writing: Alignment Coverage Matrix

Every skill the real G10 writing tests SCORE, mapped to the lesson type that TEACHES it and the item family that MEASURES it.

What this proves, and what it does not. This is a COVERAGE argument: it shows no tested skill is left un-taught or un-measured, which is NECESSARY for students to pass (you cannot pass on a skill you were never taught or tested on). It is NOT an efficacy claim: coverage does not prove the teaching works. That requires a field test with real student data, which we do not yet have. Read this as "the course targets everything the test scores," not "the course is proven to produce passing scores."
